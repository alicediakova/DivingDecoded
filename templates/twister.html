5 - Twister

These dives are initiated with the diver performing either a front, back, reverse, or inward takeoff and involve a combination of somersaults and twists…

Key components:

  • Second digit represents direction (front, back, reverse, or inward)
  • Third digit represents number of half-flips
  • Fourth digit represents number of half-twists
  • Position is always D (Free)

Example 1: “Front one-and-a-half somersaults, one-twist free”

5132D

Example 2: “Back two-and-a-half somersaults, half-twist free”

5251D
