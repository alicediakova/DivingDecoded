2 - Back

Dives that start with the number 2 are categorized as Back dives. These dives are initiated with the diver facing away from the water and involve the diver performing a backwards takeoff with backwards rotation…

Key components:

  1. Start facing away from water
  2. Backwards takeoff
  3. Flip backwards
